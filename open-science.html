Open Science

Open science is the movement to make scientific research an unhindered access transforming its reach to infinite . It empowers the open sharing of data, information, and knowledge within the scientific community and enhances wider public to accelerate scientific research and understandings.

It escalates the collaborative culture ,embracing future development in technology that holds its stakes upon opening the chattels of sciences. Open science is a commitment to stand with important questions that don't stand alone.

Why Open Science ?

Broadens participation and fosters greater collaboration in scientific investigations by lowering the barriers to entry into scientific exploration.

Generates greater impact and more citations to scientific results.

Advantages of the Open Science

Efficiency
The research would be visible to a wider audience and thus if any human errors available in the research it can be evaluated. Quality and integrity

Open access to scientific research, data and other assets will support the research process and will offer an opportunity of a broader perspective evaluation.

Open science encourages researchers to be transparent about their methods, data, and findings. This transparency makes it easier for others to scrutinize and replicate the research, promoting the reliability and reproducibility of scientific results. When research is conducted openly, it's more likely to withstand rigorous scrutiny and verification.
